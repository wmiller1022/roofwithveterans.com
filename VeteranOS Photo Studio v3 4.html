🎖 After Action Reels

Build a Before / During / After reel right from your phone. Add clips and photos, trim, label, add music, export. Keep reels around 60–90 seconds for best results.

Job: — · pull from CRM

📖 How-To Guide

🧸 Make your very first reel
1. Tap 📱 Add Clips / Photos and pick your videos and pictures from your phone.
2. They line up left-to-right in the Timeline — that's the order they'll play.
3. Tap any clip to work on it. Use ◀ ▶ to change the order.
4. Tap ▶ Preview Reel to watch it.
5. Happy? Tap 🎬 Export Video and wait for the bar to fill up. Done! 🎉
✂ Slice a video (trim it)
Tap the video in the Timeline. Play it in the little player, pause where the good part starts, and tap ✂ Set START here. Play to where it should end and tap ✂ Set END here. Or just drag the two sliders. Only the part in between goes in your reel — the original video on your phone is never changed.
🏷 Before / During / After labels
Tap a clip, then pick BEFORE, DURING, or AFTER from the label dropdown. You can pick the design (navy bar, gold badge, or clean text) and where it sits on the screen. The label gets burned into the video when you export.
◫ Side-by-side (Before next to After)
You need at least 2 clips or photos. Tap the ◫ Side-by-Side button at the end of the Timeline. Pick the LEFT one (your Before), then the RIGHT one (your After). It labels them for you automatically — tap the new split clip to change the labels.
🎵 Music & sound
Tap 🎵 Add Music and pick a song file from your phone. Slide the volume where you like it. The checkbox decides if the talking/noise from your video clips stays in or not.

⚠ Only use royalty-free music — regular radio songs will get your video muted or taken down by Facebook and Instagram.
⏩ Time lapse & speed
Tap a video clip and pick a speed. 4× or 8× turns a long tear-off into a quick time lapse. The clip's own sound turns off when sped up (so nobody sounds like a chipmunk). Try turning on Motion blur too — it makes fast motion look smooth like a movie.
🎨 Filters, text & transitions
All in the clip editor when you tap a clip:
• Filter — Black & White, Warm, Cool, Vivid, Faded, plus Brightness and Contrast sliders.
• Transition in — how this clip appears: hard cut, fade from black, or crossfade from the last clip.
• ＋ Add Text — up to 3 lines of words on any clip. Pick size, color, spot, and an optional navy or gold box.
🪄 Remove a photo background
Tap a photo clip, pick a backdrop (Navy, Parchment, Blurred, or White), and tap Go. The AI cuts out the person and puts them on the new backdrop. Works best on people, needs internet the first time, and there's always a ↩ Restore Original button if it grabs too much.
💾 Saving & coming back later
The 💾 Save button (top bar) saves your whole reel plan — order, trims, labels, text, everything. But video and music files are too big to fit inside the save file. So when you 📂 Load a project later, just tap 📱 Add Clips / Photos and pick the same videos again — the tool sees the names match and snaps them right back into place. 🧩
📐 Wide screenshots (code snippets)
Wide pictures like code screenshots get set to Fit automatically - the whole image shows with nothing chopped off. Any photo can be switched with the ⛶ button on its chip in the editor: Fit = see everything, Fill = photo fills the whole frame. For code snippets, also set that section to 1 per page so the words stay big enough to read.
🚑 If something acts up
• A file wouldn't add: it's probably HEIC/HEVC format. In your phone camera settings pick "Most Compatible", or share the video to yourself first — that converts it.
• Export takes forever: that's normal — it takes as long as the reel is. Keep the app open, screen on.
• Export failed or froze: reel's probably too long or too many big clips. Keep reels 60–90 seconds and trim clips short.
• Clip shows "re-add video": tap Add Clips and pick that file again to reconnect it.
