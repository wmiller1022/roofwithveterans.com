★ Know the Signs

Common Storm Damage

🪨 Hail Damage

Round dents or "bruises" on shingles, dented gutters, chipped paint on wood. Often invisible from ground — we climb up and look.

💨 Wind Damage

Missing, lifted, or creased shingles. Broken flashing, exposed nail heads, or shingles blown off in sections.

💧 Water Intrusion

Ceiling stains, wet insulation, mold in the attic. Water finds every crack — usually after the storm is long over.

🌲 Debris Impact

Fallen branches or trees cause structural damage to the roof deck, flashing, and ridge. Often covered by homeowners insurance.

🌀 Granule Loss

Hail strips the protective granules off shingles. Check your gutters after a storm — granule buildup is a major warning sign.

🏚️ Flashing Failure

Metal flashing around chimneys, vents, and valleys gets bent and separated by high winds, creating direct water pathways.

Not sure if you have damage? We'll tell you honestly — even if the answer is "your roof is fine."
