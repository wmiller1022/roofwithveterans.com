The Man Behind the Mission

Built on Service.
Sharpened by Experience.

Wes Miller is a Louisville, Kentucky native who has spent his adult life doing two things exceptionally well: serving and building. He served his country as a U.S. Army soldier for eight years — earning three Army Commendation Medals and graduating AIT at Fort Bliss with Honor — then spent over a decade mastering every corner of the storm restoration and roofing industry before founding Veterans Restoration of Louisville in May 2026.

He's been the guy knocking doors in June heat. He's been the estimator learning Xactimate late at night because a mentor believed in him. He's been the team leader pushing his crew to out-produce everyone around them. He's fought insurance carriers in appraisals and won. He's worn every hat in this business — and now he's putting everything he learned to work for the homeowners of Louisville.

Wes lives by a simple code: love God, love family, serve community. He attends Celebrate Recovery monthly, holds a leadership role at his local VFW post, and raises three kids alongside his partner Stephanie. He'll tell you plainly — this business wasn't built to get rich. It was built to do right by people.
