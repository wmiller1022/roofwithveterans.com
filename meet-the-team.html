★ Our Story

From Service to Rooftops

Veterans Restoration of Louisville was born from a conviction that military values belong in the trades. Our founder Wes served his country and came home looking for a way to keep serving — this time, protecting the homes of his Louisville neighbors.

What started as a small crew with a big mission has grown into Louisville's premier veteran-owned roofing and exterior restoration company. We've built our reputation one roof at a time — never cutting corners, always being straight with homeowners, and showing up every single day with the discipline that only military service teaches.

We actively hire veterans and continue to grow our team of service-minded professionals. The work changes but the mission stays the same: protect, restore, and serve.
