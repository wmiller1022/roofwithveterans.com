What Is A Marketing Partner?

Marketing Partners are customers who agree to let us share their project — photos, a yard sign, and an honest review — in exchange for a discount on their work.

1

You Have To Be A Customer

The program is only open to homeowners who complete a project with us. There's no signup without a job.

2

You Sign The Marketing Agreement

It spells out what we may share and what stays private. You approve it before anything goes public.

3

You Receive A Discount

Details are set with your rep when you sign — it depends on the scope of your project. Ask your rep for specifics.
