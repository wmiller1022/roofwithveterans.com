What Is A Roof Repair?

?

Is It Repairable?

First we check whether the material is still pliable, available, and uniform in color. If yes — a repair uses your existing material and ties cleanly into it. That's different from a full replacement, where we strip everything off.

✓

Will It Actually Fix The Problem?

That's the dilemma of repairs — you pay, and if it doesn't hold, then what? We don't take that lightly. We diagnose the real cause before we quote a fix.

★

We Are Leak Experts

We're experts at diagnosing leaks and at spotting what won't last the life of the roof. If your roof has 10 years or less left, an early repair can buy you time — wait too long and you risk paying for a repair that fails, or footing the replacement yourself.

★ Field Manual

Types Of Roof Repairs

🌊

Valleys

Valleys take a massive amount of water. We pull the shingles, add Ice & Water Shield, and tie cleanly back into your existing roof.

🔩

Flashings

Counter-flashing re-used during a past replacement won't last the life of the new roof. We can replace it — sometimes without even touching your shingles.

🧱

Chimneys

A leaking chimney could be bad flashing, a missing cricket (30"+ is code), or something not roof-related at all. You may need a tuck-point or chimney cap. We've got you.

🔧

Penetrations

Vents and pipe jacks are notorious for leaking. A repair typically means replacing the item plus roughly a 3' x 3' area around it — new IWS and new shingles.

🌪️

Tree Limb / Acts Of Nature

Something blows off, falls, gets thrown — whatever hits your roof. We do spot replacements for freak accidents and emergencies.

🔗

Tie-Ins

Adding on and need to tie into your existing roof? We tie it in and stand behind it. If it leaks, we fix it.
