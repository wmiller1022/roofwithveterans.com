★ Sit Rep

Signs You Need A Roof

  • Shingles curling, cracking, blistering, or missing
  • Granules collecting in gutters or at downspouts
  • Roof is 20+ years old
  • Daylight or water stains showing in the attic or ceilings
  • Sagging or soft spots in the roof deck
  • Damage after hail, high wind, or fallen debris
  • Repeated leaks that keep coming back

Not sure? That's what we're here for. Call us — we'll get out there and give you a straight answer.

★ Chapter: Every Roof

What Every Replacement Includes

  • Complete tear-off to the wood deck
  • Full deck inspection & cleaning — board replacement documented before & after
  • Synthetic underlayment (no old-school felt)
  • Ice & Water Shield in all valleys
  • Owens Corning starter strip on eaves & rakes
  • Pre-cut hip & ridge cap
  • New flashings — step, chimney, skylights, drip edge
  • New ventilation — ridge, box, or turbine
  • New plumbing boots & lead pipe jacks
  • Landscape protection — whatever it takes
  • Complete magnetic nail sweep of the yard
  • Six-nail install for full wind warranty

🏆 Warranty You Can Count On

Every replacement is backed by a manufacturer warranty — up to 50 years with 10-year tree protection — plus a minimum 5-year VRL workmanship warranty. When eligible, we add extended non-prorated coverage. Built right, backed in writing.
