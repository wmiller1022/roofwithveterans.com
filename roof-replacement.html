★ Is It Time?

Signs You Need A New Roof

  • Shingles are curling, cracking, or missing entirely
  • Roof is 20+ years old (asphalt shingles have a 20–30 year lifespan)
  • Granules collecting in gutters or on the ground
  • Daylight visible through the attic
  • Sagging or soft spots in the roof deck
  • Water stains on interior ceilings or walls
  • Storm damage from hail, wind, or falling debris
  • Higher energy bills from poor insulation
